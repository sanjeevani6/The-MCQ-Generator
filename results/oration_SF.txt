Q: What are some key considerations when deliberating adoption rights for LGBTQ+ individuals or couples?
A) Their financial stability
B) Their sexual orientation and gender identity
C) Their religious beliefs
D) Their geographical location
Correct Answer: Their sexual orientation and gender identity

Q: Why is it important to address the social, legal, and psychological considerations of LGBTQ+ individuals before granting them adoption rights?
A) Because they are a threat to public safety
B) Because their rights are not human rights
C) Because the child's mental health may be affected
D) Because LGBTQ+ people are not deserving of love
Correct Answer: Because the child's mental health may be affected
